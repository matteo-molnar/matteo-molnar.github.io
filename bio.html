About Matteo Molnar

Matteo Molnar was born on October 2nd, 1995.

For as far back as he can remember, he has been very interested in computers and how they work, mostly interested in the different kinds of software running on these computers. Matteo didn't really start to delve into the inner workings of software until his second last year of high school and this tinkering lead to his choice of computer science as a major in university.


Matteo's main interests are:
  • Video Games & Game Design
  • Software Development
  • Music
  • Pixel Art
  • Dungeons and Dragons
